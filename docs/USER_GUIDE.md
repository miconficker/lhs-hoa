## Getting Started

### Logging In

1. Go to the system URL (e.g., `https://lhs-hoa.pages.dev`)
2. Click **"Login"** in the top right
3. Choose your login method:
   - **Email/Password**: Enter your credentials
   - **Google Account**: Click "Sign in with Google" (recommended)
4. First-time users will be prompted to complete their profile

### Dashboard Overview

After logging in, you'll see your **Dashboard** - your personalized home base:

- **For Residents**: Quick overview of your lots, payment status, pending requests, and recent announcements
- **For Admins**: System-wide statistics, payment trends, pending verifications, and action items

### Navigation

The system has multiple ways to navigate:

#### Desktop Navigation
- **Sidebar Menu**: Click items in the left sidebar to access features
- **Global Search**: Press `Cmd/Ctrl + K` to open the command palette and search for anything
- **Quick Links**: Dashboard cards provide direct access to common tasks

#### Mobile Navigation
- **Hamburger Menu**: Tap the menu icon (☰) in the top left to open navigation
- **Bottom Navigation**: Quick access to Dashboard, Map, and Notifications
- **Swipe Gestures**: Swipe right on mobile to open sidebar

## Features for Residents

### My Profile & Settings

Access your profile from the user menu in the top right.

**What you can do:**
- View and edit your personal information
- Change your password
- Manage notification preferences
- Switch between light/dark mode

### Viewing My Lots

Go to **My Lots** from the sidebar to see all properties associated with your account.

**Information displayed:**
- Lot and block numbers
- Street address
- Ownership status (owned, rented)
- Payment status
- Associated residents

### Making Payments

Navigate to **Payments** to manage dues and fees.

#### Payment Types

| Type | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **HOA Dues** | Monthly/quarterly association fees |
| **Vehicle Pass** | Parking permit fees |
| **Employee ID** | Household help identification cards |

#### How to Make a Payment

1. Click **"Pay Now"** on the Payments page
2. Select payment type and amount
3. Choose payment method:
   - Bank transfer
   - GCash
   - Maya
   - Cash (in-person)
4. Enter reference number (from your bank/wallet)
5. Upload proof of payment (screenshot or receipt)
6. Click **Submit**

**What happens next:**
- Your payment enters the verification queue
- Administrators will review and approve/reject
- You'll receive a notification when status changes
- Approved payments appear in your payment history

### Service Requests

Submit maintenance requests or community concerns from **Service Requests**.

#### Common Request Types

- Maintenance & Repairs
- Common Area Issues
- Security Concerns
- Noise Complaints
- Facility Problems

#### Submitting a Request

1. Go to **Service Requests**
2. Click **"New Request"**
3. Fill in details:
   - **Category**: Select request type
   - **Title**: Brief description
   - **Description**: Detailed explanation
   - **Priority**: Low, Medium, or High
   - **Location**: Where the issue is (if applicable)
4. Attach photos (optional but recommended)
5. Click **Submit**

#### Tracking Status

Your requests are tracked with these statuses:
- **Open**: Submitted, not yet assigned
- **In Progress**: Being worked on
- **Resolved**: Issue fixed
- **Closed**: Request completed

### Amenity Reservations

Book community facilities from **Reservations**.

#### Available Amenities

- **Clubhouse**: Events and gatherings
- **Pool**: Swimming and recreation
- **Basketball Court**: Sports activities

#### Making a Reservation

1. Go to **Reservations**
2. Select the amenity you want to book
3. Choose your date and time slot
4. Add notes (e.g., event purpose, number of attendees)
5. Click **Book**

**Rules:**
- Maximum 4 hours per booking
- No same-day bookings
- Cancellations must be 24 hours in advance
- First-come, first-served basis

### Passes & IDs

Manage household passes from **Passes**.

#### Vehicle Passes

Register your vehicles for parking access.

**To add a vehicle pass:**
1. Go to **Passes** → **Vehicle Passes**
2. Click **"Add Vehicle"**
3. Provide:
   - Plate number
   - Vehicle make/model
   - Color
   - Owner name
4. Upload OR/CR proof
5. Submit for approval

#### Employee IDs

Create IDs for household helpers (drivers, maids, etc.).

**To add an employee:**
1. Go to **Passes** → **Employee IDs**
2. Click **"Add Employee"**
3. Provide:
   - Full name
   - Photo (clear face shot)
   - ID type (Driver's License, etc.)
   - ID number
   - Expiration date
4. Submit for approval

### Documents

Access official HOA documents from **Documents**.

**Available documents:**
- HOA Rules and Regulations
- Architectural Guidelines
- Forms and Applications
- Meeting Minutes
- Financial Reports
- Policies and Procedures

Documents are organized by category for easy browsing.

### Announcements & Events

Stay updated with community news.

#### Announcements

Official HOA communications about:
- Policy changes
- Maintenance schedules
- Emergency notices
- Community updates

#### Events

Community activities you can participate in:
- General assemblies
- Holiday celebrations
- Clean-up drives
- Social gatherings

**RSVP to events** to help planners prepare.

### Community Polls

Participate in community decisions from **Polls**.

**How polls work:**
- View active polls on the Polls page
- Read the question and options
- Select your choice
- Submit your vote
- See real-time results after voting

**Examples:**
- Budget allocations
- Rule changes
- Project priorities
- Event preferences

### Interactive Map

View the subdivision layout from **Map**.

**Map features:**
- **Lot boundaries**: Visual property lines
- **Ownership status**: Color-coded by status
  - 🟢 Green: Built/Occupied
  - 🟠 Orange: Under Construction
  - ⚪ Gray: Vacant
  - 🟣 Purple: HOA-Owned (Community Areas)
- **Block overlays**: Neighborhood boundaries
- **Household markers**: Residential locations

**Map controls:**
- **Filter by status**: Show all/built/vacant/under construction
- **Toggle layers**: Show/hide lot boundaries, block boundaries, household markers
- **Click lots**: View popup with details (address, owner, status, size)
- **Zoom/pan**: Navigate the map

**Mobile tip:** Use two-finger pinch to zoom, drag to pan.

### Messages & Notifications

#### Messages

Send direct messages to the HOA board from **Messages**.

**Use messages for:**
- Questions not covered in FAQs
- Private concerns
- Suggestions
- Report issues

#### Notifications

Stay informed with real-time updates:

**Notification types:**
- 🔔 Bell icon: View all notifications
- Toast popups: Immediate alerts for important events
- Email: Digest of recent activity (if enabled)

**You'll be notified about:**
- Payment status changes
- Service request updates
- Reservation confirmations
- New announcements

## Features for Administrators

### Admin Dashboard

Access from **Admin Panel** in the sidebar.

**Dashboard shows:**
- Total households and lots
- Payment trends and collection rates
- Active service requests
- Pending verifications
- Recent activity timeline

### Lot Management

Manage all properties from **Admin → Lots**.

**Capabilities:**
- View all lots with ownership details
- Edit lot information
- Assign owners to lots
- Update lot status (built, under construction, vacant)
- Manage lot types (residential, community, utility)
- View lot history and transfers

### Dues Configuration

Set up payment schedules from **Admin → Dues Config**.

**Configure:**
- Dues amounts by lot type
- Payment frequency (monthly, quarterly, annual)
- Due dates
- Late fee settings
- Grace periods
- Discount options (early payment)

### Payment Verification

Review submitted payments from **Admin → In-Person Payments**.

**Verification workflow:**
1. Access the verification queue
2. Review payment details and proof
3. Verify reference number matches
4. **Approve**: Payment recorded, user notified
5. **Reject**: Payment denied, user notified with reason

**Best practices:**
- Verify within 24-48 hours
- Check proof thoroughly
- Add rejection notes for clarity

### Common Areas Management

Manage shared amenities from **Common Areas**.

**Track:**
- Community facilities (clubhouse, pool, courts)
- Open spaces and parks
- Utility areas
- Usage statistics
- Maintenance schedules

### Pass Management

Approve resident applications from **Admin → Pass Management**.

**Vehicle passes:**
- Review plate number and details
- Verify OR/CR documents
- Approve/reject applications
- Issue pass numbers
- Set expiration dates

**Employee IDs:**
- Verify photo identification
- Check ID documents
- Approve/reject applications
- Generate ID cards
- Manage validity periods

### User Management

Manage resident accounts from **Admin → Users** (if enabled).

**Actions:**
- View all users
- Edit user profiles
- Assign roles (admin, resident, staff, guest)
- Reset passwords
- Suspend accounts
- View user activity

### Sending Notifications

Broadcast messages to the community from **Notifications**.

**Notification types:**
- **Announcements**: Official HOA communications
- **Alerts**: Urgent notices (weather, emergencies)
- **Reminders**: Payment deadlines, meeting notices

**Best practices:**
- Use clear, concise language
- Include action items and deadlines
- Target the right audience (all residents, specific blocks, etc.)
- Schedule important announcements in advance

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Account & Login

**Q: I forgot my password. What do I do?**
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions to reset it.

**Q: Can I use my Google account to sign in?**
A: Yes! Click "Sign in with Google" for a secure, password-free login.

**Q: How do I change my password?**
A: Go to your profile (user menu → top right) and select "Change Password."

### Payments

**Q: What payment methods are accepted?**
A: Bank transfer, GCash, Maya, and cash (in-person at the HOA office).

**Q: How long does payment verification take?**
A: Usually 24-48 hours during business days. You'll receive a notification when approved.

**Q: What if my payment is rejected?**
A: You'll receive a notification with the reason. Correct the issue and resubmit.

**Q: Can I pay for multiple lots at once?**
A: Yes, you can make separate payments for each lot you own.

### Service Requests

**Q: How do I check the status of my request?**
A: Go to Service Requests and find your request in the list. Status is shown next to each request.

**Q: What if my issue is urgent?**
A: Mark the priority as "High" when submitting. For emergencies, contact the HOA office directly by phone.

**Q: Can I cancel a service request?**
A: Yes, if it hasn't been assigned yet. Open the request and click "Cancel."

### Reservations

**Q: How far in advance can I book?**
A: Up to 30 days in advance.

**Q: What if I need to cancel my reservation?**
A: Go to Reservations, find your booking, and click "Cancel." Cancellations must be 24 hours before the booking time.

**Q: Can I book multiple time slots?**
A: Yes, but they must be on different days or non-overlapping times.

### Passes & IDs

**Q: How long does it take to get my pass/ID approved?**
A: Usually 2-3 business days after document verification.

**Q: What if my vehicle pass expires?**
A: You'll receive a notification. Renew by submitting a new application through Passes.

**Q: Can I transfer an employee ID to a new helper?**
A: No, each ID is person-specific. Submit a new application for the new employee.

### Technical Issues

**Q: The page isn't loading correctly. What do I do?**
A: Try these steps:
1. Refresh the page (F5 or Cmd+R)
2. Clear your browser cache
3. Try a different browser
4. Check your internet connection

**Q: Can I access the system on my phone?**
A: Yes! The system is fully mobile-responsive. Use your browser or add to home screen for app-like experience.

**Q: I'm not receiving notifications.**
A: Check your browser notification permissions and email settings in your profile.
